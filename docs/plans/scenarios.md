# Scenarios Plan

## What you can do...
Things entirely within the user's control right now.

- Save & Grow — increase monthly contributions
- Overpay your mortgage — reduce term and interest
- Spend Less — reduce monthly expenses
- Increase Income — pay rise or side income
- Pay off a debt — credit card, loan, car finance
- Open a Stocks & Shares ISA — start investing tax-efficiently
- Boost your pension — increase employer/personal contributions
- Build an emergency fund — how long to reach 3-6 months
- Lump sum investment — what does a windfall do invested now

---

## What can happen to you...
External events or life changes, not fully in their control.

- Income stops or reduces — redundancy, going part-time
- What if markets crashed? — 20-30% portfolio drop
- Interest rates rise — impact on mortgage payments
- Interest rates fall — impact on savings returns
- Inheritance received — lump sum impact on trajectory
- Have a child — cost and income impact
- Divorce or separation — asset split scenario
- Long-term illness — income protection gap
- Property value drops — equity impact on net worth

---

## You might wonder...
Questions users have but don't know how to frame as actions.

- Asset Runway — how long does my money last if I stop earning today
- When can I retire? — earliest possible retirement age
- Am I on track? — simple yes/no against a retirement target
- What's my FIRE number? — the pot size needed for financial independence
- How much is my mortgage costing me? — total interest over term
- What does inflation do to my savings? — real value over time
- Mortgage vs invest — which is better with my spare cash
- How long until I'm debt free? — all liabilities cleared

---

## MVP Priority

Build three from each section — nine scenarios total.

**Can do:** Save & Grow, Overpay mortgage, Boost pension

**Can happen:** Income stops, Interest rates rise, Markets crashed

**Wonder:** Asset Runway, When can I retire, Mortgage vs invest

These nine cover the questions the target user — 35-50, mortgage, pension, wants clarity — is actually lying awake thinking about. Everything else is v2.
